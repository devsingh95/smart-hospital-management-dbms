# Viva Questions and Answers (Smart Hospital Management System)

1. What is the main purpose of this project?  
Answer: The project digitizes hospital operations like patient management, doctor details, appointments, medical records, and billing.

2. Why did you choose MySQL for this project?  
Answer: MySQL is reliable, easy to use, supports relational constraints, and is suitable for DBMS academic projects.

3. Which normal form is your database in?  
Answer: The database is normalized up to Third Normal Form (3NF).

4. Why is normalization important?  
Answer: It reduces data redundancy and prevents update, insert, and delete anomalies.

5. What is the relationship between Patients and Appointments?  
Answer: One patient can have many appointments, so it is a 1:N relationship.

6. What is the use of foreign keys in your project?  
Answer: Foreign keys connect related tables and maintain referential integrity.

7. What is the purpose of the view used in your database?  
Answer: The view combines appointment, patient, and doctor details into one virtual table for easy reporting.

8. Why did you use a trigger?  
Answer: The trigger automatically generates a bill when an appointment status changes to Completed.

9. What is the use of the stored procedure in your project?  
Answer: The stored procedure encapsulates bill generation logic and avoids repetitive SQL code.

10. How can this system be improved in future?  
Answer: It can include login modules, online payments, notifications, analytics, and integration with lab/pharmacy systems.
